Admin: Add Penny Item

Only the admin account can add items here.

Admin: Find Product Images

Looks up each penny item in Dollar Tree's product catalog and saves the official product photo. Items still in the DT catalog get a real image; discontinued/clearance items that have been removed show "Image coming soon." Run once to populate, then again after adding new items.

Admin: Upload Item Photo

Upload a real product photo from your phone or computer. Overrides any catalog image. Shows "Verified item photo" on the card in Penny List and My Run.

Photos save to server storage. To persist across Render redeploys, enable the persistent disk in render.yaml (requires Starter plan — see comments in that file).

Admin: Bulk Photo Review Queue

Load a zip of screenshots or product photos, review each image one at a time, and attach clean product photos to the correct penny item. Scanner proofs, barcodes, and unusable images can be skipped or marked as proof-only. No auto-matching — you approve every attachment manually.

Admin: Bulk Import (TSV or pipe-separated)

Paste rows in format: dropDate | category | sku | description | searchTerms (tab or pipe separated). First line can be a header row — it will be skipped automatically. Duplicate SKUs are merged, not duplicated.
